Cardmarket Wants Lists Helper - What's new?

Version 1.3.0

New settings popup

A popup is available from the extension icon where you can enable or disable features, for now there is only a new one, more will come in the future.

New feature: Auto-login (optional)

Auto-login can be toggled from the popup. Credentials are stored encrypted in your browser and decrypted only by the extension when needed.
